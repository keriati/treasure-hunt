The winding path leads you to a vast corridor, illuminated by crystals pulsating with arcane energy. The Archmage fixes its ancient, wise eyes upon you, and in a voice that resonates through the very stone beneath your feet, it speaks. "Greetings, seeker of the Spellbook of Refactoring.
